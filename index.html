Privacy Policy for RightSplit

Last updated: January 1, 2026

RightSplit is designed to respect your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data

RightSplit does not collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Contacts
  • Location

You can use RightSplit without creating an account.

Usage Data

RightSplit does not collect analytics, tracking data, or usage statistics.

Financial Data

All bill amounts, items, and calculations:

  • Are stored locally on your device
  • Are never transmitted to any server
  • Are never shared with third parties

RightSplit does not access bank accounts or payment methods.

2. Purchases

RightSplit offers optional in-app purchases through Apple’s App Store.

  • Payments are processed entirely by Apple
  • RightSplit does not receive or store payment details
  • Purchase status is stored locally on your device
  • You can restore purchases using Apple’s restore mechanism

Please refer to Apple’s Privacy Policy for more information about payments.

3. Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Data is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • SplitRight does not use cloud sync or external storage

4. Data Sharing

RightSplit does not:

  • Share data with third parties
  • Sell user data
  • Use advertising SDKs
  • Use tracking technologies

5. Security

RightSplit uses Apple-provided technologies to protect your data:

  • iOS sandboxing
  • Secure device storage
  • Optional device authentication (Face ID / Touch ID, if enabled)

6. Children’s Privacy

RightSplit does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact:

Email: gymlogger.app@proton.me

9. Apple App Store Disclosure

SplitRight complies with Apple’s App Store privacy requirements and does not track users across apps or websites.

No accounts · No tracking · No ads · No cloud · Your data stays on your device
